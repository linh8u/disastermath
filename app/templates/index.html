DisasterMath

...examines the outcomes of disasters across types, severities, and countries. DisasterMath was built for cities and countries to understand how well they coped with a disaster relative to other places.

DisasterMath currently uses the EM-DAT disaster database.

And pulls data from:
World Bank data indicators to categorize a country's development level
U.S. Geological Survey for earthquake severity
Global Active Archive of Large Flood Events for flood severity
Global Volcanism Program (The Smithsonian Institute) for volcano severity
And news and government sources for wildfire severity
