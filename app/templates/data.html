There has been no open-source, comprehensive dataset regarding disasters. Additionally, hose in existence each use different standards and classifications.In an attempt to fill that void, the available dataset includes 2,110 disasters from 2000-2011. The dataset includes disasters by country, location, region, dates, killed, affected, and damages. Disaster severity and country development level has also been added.

Sources

Original dataset: EM-DAT (Centre for Research on the Epidemiology of Disasters (CRED) Earthquakes: Deaths by Year, U.S. Geological Survey Floods/Storms: Global Active Archive of Large Flood Events, Volcanoes: Global Volcanism Program (The Smithsonian Institute) Wildfires: News. Development: Health Expenditures per capita, Primary School Enrollment, GDP, Communications (World Bank Data Catalogue)
