Previously there was no open-source, comprehensive dataset available with disaster information. And, those in existence each used different standards and classifications. The dataset now made available has been curated from the USGS, Global Active Archive of Large Flood Events, the Smithsonian's Volcano data, and Wildfire: News.

The dataset also contains a Development Index composed of health expenditures per capita, primary school enrollment, GDP, and communications (Internet, mobile, landline).
