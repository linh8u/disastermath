A country's development level was calculated using health expenditures , GDP , school enrollment, and communications. Communications consists of telephone lines , mobile phone subscriptions , and internet users. An arithmetic mean was calculated using 2008 indices, and when indices were not available, the average index was substituted. Countries were then broken into five group, with 5 indicating most developed, and 1 indicating least developed.
